How does a hurricane form?

Book design

For this project, I was tasked with designing three short books based on a given piece of text explaining how a hurricane forms, each constrained to a different method of representation: (1) words only, (2) images only, and (3) a combination of words and images.

In the image-only book, I chose to work with Blenderâ€™s Geometry Nodes to generate organic, spiraling structures that abstractly model the dynamics of a hurricane, translating scientific processes into form without relying on language.
