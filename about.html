About Me

I’m Nicole Wei, originally from Beijing and currently a Communication Design junior at the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts at Washington University in St. Louis. I work at the intersection of interaction, code, 3D, and visual systems, using emerging technologies like XR and AI to explore how abstract ideas can become embodied experiences. I’m especially interested in how design can remain critical, human, and intentional as systems grow increasingly automated.
