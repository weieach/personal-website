Nightingale Bar App Design

UI / UX, Graphic design

I designed a hypothetical ordering app for Naive Imaginist, a boutique bookstore on China’s eastern coast that operates as a café by day and a bar by night. Through its Nightingale Bar concept, each beverage is inspired by a book available in-store.

The app replaces static menus with an interactive ordering experience, allowing users to explore drinks and books through visuals, narratives, and guided discovery. This transforms ordering from a transactional step into an engaging product experience.
